Biography: 

One of the most celebrated names in both American science-fiction and fantasy literature, Le Guin was born October 21st, 1929. Le Guin's career spans over 50 years of publications until her death in early 2018. She earned various awards throughout her career, including both the Hugo and Nebula Awards.
